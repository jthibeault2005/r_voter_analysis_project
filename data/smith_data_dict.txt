* ai_id: Analyst Institute unique ID
* message_treat: treatment assignment
* age: age
* race: modeled race
* gender: modeled gender
* partisanscore: score ranging from 0 - 100 predicting likelihood of supporting Democrats. Higher = more likely to support Democrats.
* outcome: survey response after treatment, only exists for those surveyed
* turnout2014: 1 if person voted in 2014 general elections, 0 otherwise
